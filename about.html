About CheckMyHack

Security insights for students, by students

Our Mission

CheckMyHack was born from a simple frustration: cybersecurity resources are either too expensive, too complex, or too disconnected from the student experience. We're building a platform where students can learn practical security skills without breaking the bank or feeling overwhelmed.

Our mission is to democratize cybersecurity education by providing accessible, hands-on learning resources that speak your language. We believe every student should have the tools to understand and defend against real-world threats.

Who I Am
Founder & Student

I'm a cybersecurity student just like you, navigating the same learning curve. I started CheckMyHack because I wanted to share the resources and insights I've gathered along the way. This isn't a corporate platform—it's a student project that grew into something bigger.

Learning Goals

As I continue my cybersecurity journey, I'm documenting everything I learn and making it available to fellow students. My current learning goals include:

  • Mastering SOC operations and SIEM tools
  • Understanding threat intelligence and analysis
  • Building practical security labs on a student budget
  • Contributing to open-source security tools
  • Sharing knowledge through workshops and briefs
